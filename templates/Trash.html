Donald Trump

Donald Trump is the 45th and current president of the United States. Before entering politics, he was a businessman and television personality.

Gun Control:

  • Trump safety commission rejects calls for gun control, sticks with suggestions that schools arm themselves.
  • The Administration opposes H.R. 8 [Bipartisan Background Checks Act of 2019] because it would impose burdensome requirements on certain firearm transactions. By overly extending the minimum time that a licensed entity is required to wait for background check results, H.R. 1112 [Enhanced Background Checks Act of 2019] would unduly impose burdensome delays on individuals seeking to purchase a firearm.

Immigration:

  • Trump's immigration policies center around six major areas:
  • Completion of the border wall with Mexico.
  • Deport immigrants who arrived in the United States as children. They are currently protected under the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program.
  • Restrict travel and work visas from eight countries.
  • Increase screening of refugees while cutting the staff needed to do so.
  • Review the H-1B visa program.
  • Curb legal immigration.
